Lemon Pie

Description

This one is my favorite, it looks impressive and tastes deliciously tart.

Let's prepare it!

Ingredients

Crust

  • 200g of Maizena crackers.
  • 150g of butter.

Filling

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk.
  • 1 can of whipping cream.
  • Juice of 4 lemons.

Icing

  • 4 egg whites.
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • Zests of 2 lemons for decoration

Steps

Crust

  1. Crumble the crackers on a blender of food processor.
  2. Add the butter and mix it.
  3. Place the dough in a pie mold.
  4. With your hands, spread the crumbles on the bottom and sides of the mold.
  5. Take it to a preheated oven at 180CÂº for 10 minutes.

Filling

  1. Blend all the ingredients until they become a smooth and firm cream.
  2. Pour the cream in the crust and take it to the fridge for 30 minutes.

Icing

  1. Whip the egg whites and add the sugar
  2. Put the icing on top of the filling.
  3. Sprinkle the lemon zests.
  4. Put it in the oven until it becomes golden brown.
